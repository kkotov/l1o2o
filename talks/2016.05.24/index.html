Problems and plans

Parsing libraries differ between online and offline

Due to the flexibility of XML, offline parsing often stumbles upon new configurations (e.g. due to missing delimiter specificator, extra whitespaces, ...)

Potential solution is a validation suite (based on offline parsing) attached to the L1CE

In addition we also need to develop a set of error codes returning to the Function Manager so as to quickly track potential problems down to the specific systems and configurations

It also helps if you let us know in advance if your new configuration needs a change to the O2O


Special thanks to Thomas and Vangelis for the constant help with the parsing tool!
